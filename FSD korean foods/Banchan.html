Banchan

A collection of small Korean side dishes served with rice.

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Pickled vegetables
  • Seasoned spinach
  • Sautéed bean sprouts
  • Fish cakes
  • Pajeon (green onion pancake)

Instructions

  1. Prepare small portions of each dish as side items.
  2. For kimchi, ferment cabbage with chili and garlic.
  3. For pickled vegetables, use radish or cucumber and marinate in vinegar and sugar.
  4. For seasoned spinach, sauté with sesame oil and garlic.
  5. Serve the dishes alongside rice and enjoy!
