Samgyetang

A delicious Korean ginseng chicken soup

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Ginseng roots
  • Garlic
  • Jujube (Korean dates)
  • Rice
  • Ginger
  • Ginseng extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Clean the chicken and stuff with rice, garlic, and ginseng roots.
  2. Place chicken in a pot and add water, ginger, and jujube.
  3. Simmer for 1-2 hours until the chicken is tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
