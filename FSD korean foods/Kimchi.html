Kimchi

A popular Korean dish made with fermented cabbage and chili paste.

Ingredients

  • Chinese cabbage
  • Salt
  • Gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spring onions

Instructions

  1. Cut cabbage and salt it for 2-3 hours until it wilts.
  2. Make a paste with garlic, ginger, gochugaru, fish sauce, and sugar.
  3. Rinse the cabbage and mix it with the paste, making sure all leaves are covered.
  4. Store in an airtight container and ferment at room temperature for 1-3 days.
  5. Refrigerate after fermentation and serve with rice or other Korean dishes.
