Bibimbap

A savory Korean mixed rice dish with vegetables, meat, and a fried egg, topped with gochujang (chili paste).

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Spinach
  • Carrot
  • Bean sprouts
  • Egg (fried)
  • Beef (or tofu for vegetarian version)
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Prepare rice and vegetables by sautéing each one separately in sesame oil.
  2. Cook the beef (or tofu) and slice thinly.
  3. Place rice in a bowl and top with cooked vegetables, meat, and a fried egg.
  4. Drizzle gochujang and sesame oil on top and garnish with sesame seeds.
  5. Mix everything together and serve hot.
