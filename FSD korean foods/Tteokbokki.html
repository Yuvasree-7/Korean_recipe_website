Tteokbokki

A spicy and savory Korean street food made with chewy rice cakes.

Ingredients

  • Rice cakes (tteok)
  • Korean fish cakes
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Sesame oil
  • Spring onions

Instructions

  1. Soak rice cakes in water for 30 minutes if they are hard.
  2. In a pan, sauté garlic in sesame oil and add fish cakes and rice cakes.
  3. Mix gochujang, gochugaru, soy sauce, and sugar in water to make the sauce.
  4. Pour the sauce over the rice cakes and simmer until they become tender and the sauce thickens.
  5. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
