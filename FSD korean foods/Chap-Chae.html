Chap Chae

A savory stir-fried noodle dish with vegetables and meat.

Ingredients

  • Sweet potato noodles (dangmyeon)
  • Beef (or chicken or pork)
  • Spinach
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms (shiitake or button mushrooms)
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the sweet potato noodles according to the package instructions and set aside.
  2. Sauté beef (or your choice of meat) with garlic in sesame oil until browned.
  3. Stir-fry carrots, onions, mushrooms, and spinach separately in sesame oil.
  4. Combine all vegetables and meat with the cooked noodles in a large pan.
  5. Add soy sauce, sugar, and black pepper and stir-fry until well combined.
  6. Serve hot with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
