Gimbap

A popular Korean dish made with rice and various fillings wrapped in seaweed.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Seaweed sheets
  • Carrot
  • Spinach
  • Egg
  • Cucumber
  • Pickled radish
  • Beef or imitation crab (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare all ingredients by cooking rice and cutting vegetables into thin strips.
  2. Place a seaweed sheet on a bamboo mat and spread a thin layer of rice on top.
  3. Place vegetables and optional meat on top of the rice.
  4. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat and slice into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Serve with dipping sauce or kimchi.
